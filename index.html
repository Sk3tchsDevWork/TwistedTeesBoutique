Shipping Information

Free Shipping

Free standard shipping on all orders over $75 within the US. Use code FREESHIP75 at checkout.

Shipping Options

  • Standard (5-7 business days): $5.99
  • Express (2-3 business days): $12.99
  • Overnight (1 business day): $24.99

International Shipping

We ship worldwide! International shipping rates calculated at checkout. Delivery times vary by location (7-21 business days).

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

Returns & Exchanges

30-Day Return Policy

We offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached.

How to Return

  1. Contact us at returns@twistedteesboutique.com
  2. We'll send you a prepaid return label
  3. Pack items securely and attach the label
  4. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We offer free exchanges within the US. International customers receive store credit for exchanges.

Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return. You'll get an email confirmation when it's complete.

Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use?

We use premium cotton blends, organic materials, and sustainable fabrics. Each product page lists specific material details.

How do I care for my items?

Machine wash cold, tumble dry low. Avoid bleach and iron inside-out to preserve prints. Care instructions are on each garment tag.

Do you restock sold-out items?

Popular items are usually restocked within 2-4 weeks. Sign up for restock notifications on product pages to be notified first.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, contact us and we'll do our best to help.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes! We offer wholesale pricing for orders of 20+ pieces. Contact us at wholesale@twistedteesboutique.com for details.

Are your products ethically made?

Absolutely! We partner with certified ethical manufacturers and are committed to fair labor practices and sustainable production.
